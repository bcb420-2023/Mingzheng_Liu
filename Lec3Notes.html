Packages required

Geometadb

  • a package that make querying the metadata describing microarray experiments, platforms, and datasets easier. Especially for R users.
  • Based on a SQLite database; GEO datatypes including: ** GEO samples (GSM) ** GEO platforms (GPL) ** GEO data series (GSE) curated GEO datasets (GDS)
